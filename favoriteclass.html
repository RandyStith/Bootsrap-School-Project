Info about chemistry

Chemistry is the scientific study of the properties and behavior of matter. It is a natural science that covers the elements that make up matter to the compounds composed of atoms, molecules and ions: their composition, structure, properties, behavior and the changes they undergo during a reaction with other substances. Chemistry is one of the most crucial sciences, as it plays a role in almost all others. In biology, your body is made up of atoms, BOOM. Space sciences, our sun's energy is made from billions and billions of nuclear reactions, BOOM again. Archaeology, bones get fossilised when pressure along with sediments cause chemical changes in the structure of bones, BOOM.

Why Is Chemistry My Favorite Class?

Chemistry is my favorite class because unlike other classes that are either easy or informational, chemistry is both. I have learned so much interesting things in chemistry, like how acid rain works, and how our stomach acid can break down gold, but not lithium. All of these things make Chemistry so much more fascinating to me. I also enjoy having the double periods that you get in honors chem. It is like a double period of the best class.
