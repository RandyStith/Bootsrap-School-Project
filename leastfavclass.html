History Advanced













Info about History

History is essentially the study of past events. There are categories of History so that any time period can be your expertise. In FTHS the main road of history classes is World History, US history, then World history II or US history II. I am currently in Advanced US history, the reason it is advanced is because I was in Honors last year and was recommended for advanced. History is a crucial part in understanding government systems and ideaologies that you may want to pursue in the future.

Why Is History My Least Favorite Class?

The reason that History is currently my least favorite class is because I find the ways we are learning to be very boring and laborious. While I don't mind the topics that much, the ubiquitous note taking and packets make me loathe the whole class. The teacher is perfectly fine, their ways of teaching are slightly confusing, but overall they are nice and relatively accomodative. So I don't dislike History or my teacher, but the class is my least favorite because of the way we are being taught.
