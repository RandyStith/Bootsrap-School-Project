How to read the shedule

At Freehold Township Highschool, the schedules are pretty complex. But once you understand them they are a breeze to understand.
The top bar shows which day of the cycle that the below times and classes correspond to. The left side shows the times that the classes start and end.
The Letter classes cycle every seven days of school. so the first day would be and "A" day then "B", "C", etc.
As you can see the room numbers and teacher's names are on the classe's squares. They are colored to help visualise the rotation of classes every day.


Summary Of My Classes

I have 7 classes:
Spanish, Physical Education, Chemistry, Web Design, Algebra, English, and History.
Spanish is one of my easiest classes, I find languages pretty fascinating, and I know that Spanish is a popular language that can grant me opportunities.
Phys Ed is fun and lets me get my energy out early in the day. Safety is a sub-class that I have this year, it is basically driver's ed and I have learned quite a lot.
Chemistry is not as easy as Spanish, but I am actually learnign things that I see myself using or at least stuff that I think is interesting.
Web Design is the class that I learned to make this website in. It is a lot of fun and not very difficult, as long as you pay attention.
Algebra is one of my harder classes but is one that I have always enjoyed none the less.
English is a class that isn't very difficult, but I find it very boring. I enjoy creative writing but having to cite and find quotes is very laborious.
History is pretty difficult for me, I kinda enjoy learning about history, but the class is very monotonous and boring.
